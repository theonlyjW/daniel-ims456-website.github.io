HUMAN TRAFFICKING









Human trafficking involves the illegal trade of people, often for forced labor or commercial sexual exploitation. Victims are deceived, coerced, or force into situations where their freedom is exploited for the profit of traffickers. It's a grave violation of human rights and a global concern.
